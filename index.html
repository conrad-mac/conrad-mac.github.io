About Me

I am a highly motivated analyst with more than five years experience in New Zealand's public sector. I am passionate about exploring, analysing and solving problems with data of all sorts! I have experience and proficiency using a wide range of analytical software (R, SAS, SQL, Python, etc.).

I graduated from Victoria University of Wellington in 2010 with a Bachelor of Science, majoring in geography. As well as geography, I also completed papers in statistics and political science.

I have worked as an analyst at Statistics New Zealand and the Ministry of Social Development and am currently on secondment to the Social Investment Unit (formerly the Social Sector Investment Change Program) as a Senior Analyst. I am part of a team that works daily with Statsistics New Zealand's Integrated Data Infrastructure (IDI).
